# Lunzy (LNZ) – Community-Driven Meme Token

**Tagline:** Entertainment, Community, and the Journey – A Meme Token Growing Together with Its Community

---

## Project Overview
Lunzy is a community-driven meme cryptocurrency built on the **Avalanche C-Chain (AVAX)**.  
Designed with a fixed supply and an **immutable smart contract**, Lunzy focuses on **transparency, security, and long-term sustainability**.  

Instead of relying on unrealistic promises, Lunzy grows together with its community, fostering creativity, participation, and collective development.

---

## Key Features

- **Immutable & Ownerless:** No privileged roles, no minting, no admin control.  
- **Zero Transaction Fees:** Fully tax-free wallet-to-wallet and DEX transactions.  
- **Transparent Tokenomics:** Supply and burn mechanisms are verifiable on-chain.  
- **Community-First Approach:** Decisions are guided by community engagement and feedback.  
- **Security-Oriented:** ERC20 standard with EIP-2612 permit support and ERC165 interface detection.

## Token Distribution 
- **Liquidity (20%)**: Portion locked to stabilize the market; remainder held as a reserve for controlled use, including optional burns.  
- **Community (10%)**: Released gradually through a controlled vesting schedule to support ecosystem adoption.  
- **Marketing (15%)**: Gradually released via predictable vesting for long-term project promotion.  
- **Partner / Influencer (15%)**: Managed per individual agreements with structured release.  
- **Team (15%)**: Daily or periodic controlled releases under vesting to ensure operational stability.  
- **Reserve (25%)**: Controlled and gradual release supporting ecosystem growth.  

_All allocations follow transparent, controlled, and sustainable vesting mechanisms._

---

## Burn Procedures

Lunzy implements pre-planned, transparent burn processes independent of transaction fees:

- **LNZ Burns:** Sent to irreversible burn addresses via controlled releases.  
- **LUNC Burns:** Managed manually through private wallets with scheduled burns.  
- **Liquidity Pool Burns:** Tokens may be used for burns based on market conditions.  

_All burn operations are irreversible and fully verifiable on-chain._
